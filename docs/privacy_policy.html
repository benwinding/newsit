Privacy Policy

Newsit is a Chrome browser extension developed by Ben Winding.

Data Collection

Newsit does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

The extension temporarily accesses the current webpage URL and title within your browser solely to generate links to Hacker News and Reddit. This information is processed locally and is never collected or stored.

Data Usage

Any accessed information (such as the current page URL) is used only to provide the core functionality of the extension. This data is not used for tracking, analytics, or any other purpose.

Data Storage

Newsit does not store any user data locally or remotely.

Data Sharing

Newsit does not share any user data with third parties.

Third-Party Services

Newsit does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.

Cookies

Newsit does not use cookies or any tracking technologies.

Security

Since Newsit does not collect or store personal data, no personal information is exposed or retained.

External Links

Newsit may direct users to external websites such as Hacker News or Reddit. These sites operate independently and have their own privacy policies.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.
