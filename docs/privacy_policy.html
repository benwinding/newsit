Privacy Policy

Newsit is a Chrome browser extension developed by Ben Winding and provided free of charge. This extension is intended for use as-is.

No Data Collection

Newsit does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information or user data.

The extension does not collect:

  • Personal information
  • Browsing history
  • Website content
  • Analytics or usage data
  • Identifiers or tracking information

How the Extension Works

Newsit generates quick links to discussions on Hacker News and Reddit related to the webpage currently being viewed.

To perform this function, the extension temporarily accesses the current page URL and title within your browser. This information is processed locally on your device only and is never transmitted to any external server.

Data Storage

Newsit does not store any user data locally or remotely. All processing occurs entirely within the user's browser session.

Third-Party Services

Newsit does not use analytics providers, advertising services, tracking technologies, or third-party data processors.

Cookies

Newsit does not use cookies or any tracking technologies.

Data Sharing

No user data is collected or shared with any third parties.

Security

Because Newsit does not collect or transmit user data, there is no personal information stored or exposed by the extension.

Links to External Sites

The extension may open external websites such as Hacker News or Reddit. These websites operate independently and have their own privacy policies.

Children’s Privacy

Newsit does not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
